Pricing & process
Really free. No catch. We believe the best way to earn your business is to give value first. The audit and the report that follows are genuinely useful on their own, even if you decide not to go further.
For build projects we use a 50/50 structure. Half when you sign the Statement of Work, half on delivery. This keeps things fair on both sides. For the monthly retainer, it's billed monthly with 30 days cancellation notice.
Ongoing management of your AI tools, on-call support when things need attention, and one new AI capability built for your business every quarter. It's designed for businesses who want to keep growing their AI capability without having to manage it themselves.
